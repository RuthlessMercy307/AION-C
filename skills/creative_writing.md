Creative writing approach.

- Story = character + desire + obstacle + change.
- Open with concrete sensory detail, not abstract setup.
- Show, don't tell: actions and dialogue over narration of feelings.
- Tension comes from what the character wants vs what they get.
- Cut every word that doesn't earn its place.
- One strong image beats five weak ones.
- Vary sentence length to control pace: short for impact, long for flow.
- End on a turn: a small revelation or shift, not a summary.
- Read aloud to catch rhythm and stumble.
