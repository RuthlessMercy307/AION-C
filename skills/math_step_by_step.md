Math problem-solving approach.

- Restate the problem in your own words. Identify what is asked.
- Identify the given values and the unknowns.
- Choose a method or formula. Justify why.
- Show each step of the calculation, one transformation per line.
- Substitute numerical values only at the end when possible.
- Verify the answer:
  - Plug it back into the original equation
  - Check the units match
  - Check the order of magnitude is reasonable
- If multiple methods work, mention the alternative briefly.
- Round only at the final step. Keep precision in intermediates.
